Wei Li (William)

Undergraduate Researcher, Shanghai University

I am an undergraduate researcher in Computer Science at Shanghai University (Project 211), with an expected graduation in June 2027.

My research interests lie at the intersection of Deep Learning, AI, Time Series, and HCI. Under the mentorship of Prof. Fenglei Yang, I focus on developing robust, efficient, and interpretable models for multi-scale temporal dynamics. My work has resulted in multiple sole-author and first-author publications accepted for Oral Presentations at several international conferences.
